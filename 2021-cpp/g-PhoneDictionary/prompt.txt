On older mobile devices, numbers were mapped to letters:

2 - A B C
3 - D E F
4 - G H I
5 - J K L
6 - M N O
7 - P Q R S
8 - T U V
9 - W X Y Z

Given a sequence of numbers, return the possible permutations of those numbers
that are present in a dictionary.

For example,

Inputer sequence: 232
Dictionary: AEC, WUT, BAD, OK, AAAA, CFC

Output: AEC CFC
